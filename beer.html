A Craft Brewery

In the heart of Värmland lies Fryksdalen, a deep crack valley in the landscape. And so maybe life is a tear in everything beautiful. Inside the Sunne, a stone's throw from the Fryken, the brewery is located in an old large kitchen from the 1940s.

Details and cold rooms are intact and the solid doors can measure themselves with bank vaults. In 2014, we renovated the premises and replaced the steam pots and dish rooms with breweries and yeast tanks.
